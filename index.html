Healing Journal & 7‑Day Affirmations

A guided journal helps you reflect, track growth, and practice repair. Combine it with our 7-day affirmation plan to create momentum in your relationship.

Sample Daily Template

  1. Morning: One-sentence intention for the relationship today.
  2. Midday (2 min): What went well? What needs attention?
  3. Evening (5–10 min): One appreciation, one learning, one action for tomorrow.
  4. Conflict Log: What happened, how I felt, what I need, repair steps.

Want a printable PDF of the full healing journal? Use the Contact form to request the sample and we'll send it.

7‑Day Affirmations

  • Day 1 — Rooting: "We choose presence. I will listen with care and return to connection."
  • Day 2 — Honesty: "I speak my truth with kindness. My partner’s truth matters."
  • Day 3 — Repair: "It’s safe to pause, to say sorry, and to rebuild."
  • Day 4 — Gratitude: "We notice the small things that lift us."
  • Day 5 — Courage: "I face hard conversations because connection matters."
  • Day 6 — Play: "Joy is part of our healing. We make space to laugh."
  • Day 7 — Commitment: "We choose each other, again and again."
